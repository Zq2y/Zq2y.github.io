I am a PhD student in Statistics at the Institute of Statistics and Big Data, Renmin University of China, under the direction of Assistant Professor Yuqian Zhang. I received my bachelor’s degree from the School of School of Mathematical Sciences at Beijing Normal University in 2024.

My research focuses on theory and methodology in causal inference, semi-supervised learning and high-dimensional statistics.
